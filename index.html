Welcome to Celie's Place!

We are a family-owned establishment, and have been proud to serve the Cumby area since 1985. Specializing in southern comfort dishes, we pride ourselves on providing our community with homecooked meals "just like Mama used to make"! All of our ingredients are locally sourced and every meal is prepared fresh daily. We don't just offer family meals - we offer a family experience. Come let us warm your heart and fill your belly!
